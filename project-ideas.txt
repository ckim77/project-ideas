Project:
- create an app like twitter except it revolves around basketball content

MVP/Features:
- Users have their own accounts (login/password)
- Able to put up posts of recent games or players
- Users can write posts
- Users can delete/read posts
- Users can look up NBA players using API
- Posts will be saved to a database

Extra features:
- able to create own teams of 5 players and play a simulation match with other teams
- upload videos of highlights
